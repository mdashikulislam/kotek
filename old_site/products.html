       Comprensive Steering Product Listing

Kotek offers the most comprehensive range of power steering component product lines in the remanufacturing industry. Whether you need a high pressure seal for a domestic vehicle or a boot seal for an import, we got you covered. We are backed by 46+ years of OE Sealing Systems expertise and TS16949 / ISO 9001 certified manufacturing facilities. At the Kotek California research and testing facilities, seals are installed on actual units and tested for performance and endurance to match or improve on OE. Working with our customer, our power steering seal designers created a special set of seals tailored to salvaging otherwise unusable cores. At Kotek, we go the extra mile to create quality product lines that you can trust and depend on.
